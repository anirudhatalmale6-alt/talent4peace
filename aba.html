About ABA

Asosyasyon Blogè Ayisyen

ABA (Asosyasyon Blogè Ayisyen) is the official network of Haitian bloggers, content creators, and digital media voices operating inside the Talent4Peace ecosystem. Founded to unite, empower, and amplify Haiti's digital storytellers, ABA provides structure, visibility, and real opportunities to creators who are shaping the narrative of Haiti.


Whether you are a blogger covering local news, a YouTuber showcasing Haitian culture, an influencer driving community engagement, or a media team covering events - ABA gives you a platform, a network, and a voice that matters. As part of the HaitiBiznis ecosystem, ABA members gain access to the full suite of tools, audiences, and partnerships across all connected platforms.
